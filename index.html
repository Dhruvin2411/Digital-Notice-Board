Why Digital Notice Board?

Traditional notice boards are limited to specific places and times. Our Digital Notice Board ensures that students and faculty can access important information instantly, whether on their laptops, tablets, or mobile phones.

  • Easy access to latest notices
  • Mobile friendly design
  • Eco-friendly (no paper required)
